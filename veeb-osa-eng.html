About Me

Hello! I am Karl Martin Hollo, a photographer who loves capturing moments and beauty through the camera lens. My journey in photography began nearly 5 years ago when I borrowed my sister's camera. I instantly enjoyed the charm of a manual camera, which gave me the opportunity to capture everything my soul desired. My photography path has been diverse, but for the past 2 years, I have dedicated myself to automotive photography. This genre has become my passion, and I have attended several workshops to refine my skills and the quality of my work. Additionally, I regularly receive feedback that helps me continually improve my work. My website is a place where I want to share my best works and experiences. Currently, I consider my best works to be the Porsche 911 and BMW 1500 which were photographed during the Motoshow in Tartu. Also, my website contains many other interesting captures that I have documented.
Welcome to my world, where photos tell their own story!
